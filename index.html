Frequently Asked Questions

How does Layer Management work?
Layers blend from top to bottom. Click any layer item in the list to select it as active. Use the eye icon to toggle visibility, the clone icon to duplicate canvas buffers, and the trash icon to delete layers.
Why does the exported image look clean and crisp?
The engine renders pixels dynamically onto an unscaled buffer. Upon export, the image automatically upscales 16x using non-destructive bilinear filtering overrides, keeping pixel boundaries perfect for digital sharing.
Can I alter the viewport background?
Yes. Locate the Canvas Background card block within the right sidebar panel to toggle between a standard alpha transparency checkerboard grid, solid dark velvet black, or clean white.
